# Product Context

## What is Pixel?
Pixel is a collaborative digital canvas platform where users can purchase permanent pixels and create lasting artwork. The platform features a competitive "Throne" system where users bid for the coveted center position at coordinates (0,0).

## Core Value Propositions
1. **Permanent Ownership** - Unlike social media posts that disappear, pixels are yours forever
2. **Real-Time Competition** - Battle for the Throne at 0,0, the most visible spot on the canvas
3. **Growing Value** - Strategic positioning matters as the canvas fills up
4. **Community Creation** - Collaborative artwork and shared digital history

## User Journey
1. **Discover** - Land on homepage, see live canvas activity, understand the concept
2. **Explore** - Browse the canvas in guest mode, see other creations
3. **Convert** - Sign up and purchase credits (starting at $5 for 50 pixels)
4. **Create** - Place pixels, build artwork, claim territory
5. **Compete** - Challenge for the Throne, climb leaderboards
6. **Community** - Share creations, collaborate, join Discord

## Pricing Model
- **1 Credit = 1 Pixel** (simple, transparent)
- **Packages:**
  - Starter: 50 credits / $5 ($0.10/pixel)
  - Creator: 200 credits / $15 ($0.075/pixel) - Most Popular
  - Artist: 500 credits / $40 ($0.08/pixel)
  - Master: 2,000 credits / $100 ($0.05/pixel) - Best Value
- **Zone Multipliers:**
  - Zone 0 (Focal Point): 10x
  - Zone 1 (Inner Ring): 3x
  - Zone 2 (Middle Ring): 1.5x
  - Zone 3 (Outer Ring): 1x (base price)

## Throne Mechanics
- Minimum bid: +20% above current value
- 24-hour grace period for current holder to counter
- Defender advantage on ties
- Permanent Hall of Fame record
- Special badges and recognition for holders

## Target Audience
- Digital artists and pixel art enthusiasts
- Collectors seeking unique digital ownership
- Brands wanting permanent online presence
- Gaming communities and internet culture participants
- Anyone wanting to leave a permanent mark on the internet
